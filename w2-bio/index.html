About Me

Over winter, I go skiing with my family. We ski at Okemo Mountain, in Vermont. In February, my family and I are going on a trip to Colorado to go skiing. We are going with our family friends, who ski with us every weekend, and we have skied with them for over ten years. Sometimes, my family friends and I wear onesies of various characters from movies and shows, like Olaf from Frozen. Skiing makes me happy, especially when I see the view of the mountains and skyline.
